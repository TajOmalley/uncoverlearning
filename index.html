our mission

Use AI developments to build software that makes teaching and studying more personalized. Train models on open source textbooks to make our platform affordable and empower professors and students to improve outcomes globally.

our solution

A course-based education platform that improves outcomes with AI-powered teaching content and personalized tutoring—all based on educator–selected open source materials.
