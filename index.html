our mission

Use developments in AI to build software that makes teaching and learning more personalized. Train AI models on a library of open source textbooks to make the platform affordable. Empower professors and students to improve learning outcomes with accessibility across college types and internationally.

the problem

College textbooks are over-priced and ineffective. Publishers serve professors, not students—resulting in expensive textbooks that fail to support effective studying.

our solution

A course-based education platform that improves student outcomes through AI-powered teaching content and personalized tutoring. Our AI models guide learning using professor-selected open source materials.
